EVERYTHING TO KNOW ABOUT
MEDHA 2022

THEME

GIET brings you MEDHA 2022 with
AI - ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE "A Genealogy of power" as its theme for this year's Engineer's day i.e., 14th & 15th SEPTEMBER to celebrate engineering and the contribution of the world's engineers to a better, sustainable world, events correlating with various fields of engineering are conducted through which the upcoming engineers can showcase their skills and win cash prizes upto 3 Lakhs.

REGISTRATION FEE

  • Payment for event registrations are offline.
  • Registration fee for participation: 100/-
  • Pay once and participate in any number of events (Spot events excluded)

STEPS TO REGISTER

  • Reach events section.
  • Click on the event you want to register.
  • After being redirected to event rules And instructions page, Scroll down and click on register button.
  • Fill the form and click on submit.
  • Wait for 24 hours to recieve registration confirmation and an unique code to your registered mail id.

HELP DESK

  • At the day of the events, an offline Help desk will be arranged for participants.
  • Any queries regarding registrations and events, Help desk can be contacted near GIET globe.
  • Accommodation for other college students will be provided during the event time, i.e., 14th & 15th September.
