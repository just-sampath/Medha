    • RULES AND REGULATIONS:
  • ID card is mandatory.
  • Time management will be strictly followed.
  • Any act against the spirit of the event, the participant will be disqualified without question.
  • The competition is open only to undergraduate students.
  • Members should have a valid student ID card from their college.
  • A team member can participate in only one project presentation.
  • Commercially prepared kits or models cannot constitute a major portion of the project.
  • Each team can have a maximum of 4 members.
  • Students are solely responsible for the security and safety of their equipment.
  • There are no restrictions on the number of teams from the same college.
  • Students must set up their project, remain with it throughout their judging period, and remove it at the end of the day.
  • No hazardous, toxic or flammable materials are to be used. Candles, Bunsen burners and electrical heating devices (i.e., hot plates) are not allowed.
  • Standard laboratory safety rules must be observed.
  • Teams will be given a space to set up their model/design at the venue for exhibition and for judging.
  • For development/software related projects, originality of the work is mandatory.
